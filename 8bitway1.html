8-Bit Way

This is CarryOn and Whipinsnapper's first custom track. This track was made in five days and submitted for the 15th edition of the Custom Track Jam competition series, where it placed tenth. It takes place in an arcade where racers explore different retro games, namely Pac-Man and Tetris. CarryOn made the course model in Blender and worked on the KMP, while Whipinsnapper worked on the KMP and KCL files, added vertex colors, made the minimap, and made some slight changes to the course model.
